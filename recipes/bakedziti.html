Baked Ziti

Description:

Baked Ziti is a pasta that uses ziti pasta, ground beef, and lots and lots of yummy cheeses. We got your provolone, we got your mozzarella, we even got your parmesan cheese. It's good recipe for the pasta loving cats out there.

The recipe is not a hard one to make and doesn't take much time either, depending how good you're in the kitchen of course. Takes about 15 minutes to prep and another 45 minutes to cook. The recipe also yields a whopping 1 9x13-inch casserole and has a serving size of about ten. If you're looking for more then just double up the recipe.
